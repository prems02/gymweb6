What is Weightlifting?

Weightlifting is a form of strength training that involves lifting weights to improve muscle strength, size, and endurance. It includes various exercises that target different muscle groups using equipment such as dumbbells, barbells, and weight machines.

Common weightlifting exercises include squats, deadlifts, bench presses, and overhead presses. Weightlifting is not only used for building muscle but also for enhancing overall fitness and athletic performance.
