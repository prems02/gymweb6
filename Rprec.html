Precautions for Running

When running, consider the following precautions to ensure a safe and effective experience:

  1. Wear appropriate running shoes that provide support and cushioning.
  2. Warm up before starting your run to prepare your muscles and joints.
  3. Start with shorter distances and gradually increase your mileage to avoid overuse injuries.
  4. Stay hydrated and maintain a balanced diet to support your running routine.
  5. Listen to your body and rest if you experience pain or discomfort.
