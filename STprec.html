Precautions for Strength Training

To ensure a safe and effective strength training routine, keep these precautions in mind:

  1. Warm up before starting to prepare your muscles and joints.
  2. Use proper form to prevent injuries and maximize effectiveness.
  3. Start with lighter weights and gradually increase as you build strength.
