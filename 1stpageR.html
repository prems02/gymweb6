What is Running?

Running is a popular form of cardiovascular exercise where individuals move at a pace faster than walking, typically over longer distances. It involves repetitive leg movements and engages various muscle groups, including the legs, core, and arms.

Running can be done outdoors on trails, tracks, or roads, or indoors on treadmills. It's a versatile activity that can be adapted to different fitness levels and goals.
